Help Center

Use these resources to get clear on MOQ, artwork, sampling, shipping, and what to prepare before you contact the factory.

Before you sample

Choose a focused category, shortlist prints and fabrics, and use the catalog and base styles pages to narrow your first range.

Before you approve artwork

Check print scale on the actual garment, confirm color direction and placement before sample cutting, and flag trims/labels/packaging needs early.

Before you ship

Decide FOB, CIF, or DDP before final quoting. Confirm delivery destination and target in-store date. Review packing/carton expectations before bulk closes.
