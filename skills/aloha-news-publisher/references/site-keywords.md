# Site Keyword Clusters

Choose one cluster per daily batch. A source must connect to at least one accepted angle in the selected cluster.

## resort wear manufacturing

Buyer intent: founders and wholesale teams planning dresses, shirts, cover-ups, sets, and resort capsules.

Accepted angles:

- resort capsule planning
- wholesale assortment shifts
- vacation retail demand
- fabric selection for warm-weather apparel
- size grading, fit, and production readiness
- factory-direct sourcing and line development

Sample topics:

- resort wear manufacturer
- private label resortwear
- wholesale resort dresses
- aloha shirts manufacturing
- matching family resort sets

## swimwear and UPF50+ activewear

Buyer intent: teams sourcing swim, rash guards, beach activewear, and sun-protection styles.

Accepted angles:

- swim silhouettes and coverage
- UPF or sun-protection fabric demand
- rash guard, surf, and active swim details
- stretch recovery, opacity, lining, and fit
- recycled or performance yarns
- swim capsule planning and size range

Sample topics:

- private label swimwear manufacturer
- UPF50 activewear
- rash guard manufacturing
- custom swimwear
- resort swim capsule

## low MOQ sampling

Buyer intent: early-stage brands or retailers testing product before bulk orders.

Accepted angles:

- smaller production runs
- sampling speed and fit approval
- capsule testing
- inventory risk reduction
- boutique wholesale launches
- pre-order or demand testing

Sample topics:

- low MOQ resort wear
- low MOQ swimwear
- apparel sampling
- private label startup production
- sample to bulk workflow

## custom prints

Buyer intent: brands building distinctive artwork, destination prints, or private-label collections.

Accepted angles:

- print trend signals
- artwork scaling across silhouettes
- digital print, screen print, placement print, or repeat print
- color matching and trim coordination
- destination-specific merchandising
- IP-safe original artwork

Sample topics:

- custom print resort wear
- aloha shirt prints
- swimwear print development
- resort textile artwork
- private label print program

## FOB/DDP shipping

Buyer intent: sourcing managers and founders comparing landed-cost and delivery options.

Accepted angles:

- freight rate shifts
- tariff, duty, or customs changes
- port disruption or lead-time risk
- FOB, CIF, DDP, and landed-cost decisions
- production calendar planning
- supplier communication around delivery windows

Sample topics:

- DDP shipping apparel
- FOB garment sourcing
- apparel landed cost
- resortwear shipping
- China apparel logistics

## private label trims and packaging

Buyer intent: brands turning generic styles into retail-ready private-label products.

Accepted angles:

- woven labels, hangtags, packaging, and care labels
- compliance labeling
- trims affecting minimums and timelines
- premium retail presentation
- SKU discipline for small collections

Sample topics:

- private label apparel trims
- resortwear branding
- swimwear hangtags
- custom care labels
- boutique packaging
